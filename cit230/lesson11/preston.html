Playing in the Snow

by Kyle Brazelton

Sometimes you just need to play with a puppy in the snow. Here at Weather Hunter we keep constant updates so that you know when is a good time to take your puppy out for a stroll in the snow, or weather you need to go buy some food and water to bunker down for the next blizzard which could hit at anytime! Severe weather can happen anytime, in any part of the country. Severe weather can include hazardous conditions produced by thunderstorms, including damaging winds, tornadoes, large hail, flooding and flash flooding, and winter storms associated with freezing rain, sleet, snow and strong winds. Lightning is a leading cause of injury and death from weather-related hazards. Although most lightning victims survive, people struck by lightning often report a variety of long-term, debilitating symptoms. Failing to evacuate flooded areas, entering flood waters, or remaining after a flood has passed can result in injury or death. Flooding is a temporary overflow of water onto land that is normally dry. Tornadoes can destroy buildings, flip cars, and create deadly flying debris. Tornadoes are violently rotating columns of air that extend from a thunderstorm to the ground. Floods are the most common natural disaster in the United States. So it's best to be prepared by making sure you are stocked up with your dialy necessities- remember you will eventually be able to get out and go to the store so hording isn't necessary, but having a good supply for about 2 weeks is always a good idea. This includes water, food, shampoo and other bathroom essentials, and of course, food and supplies for your pets!   continue to article
