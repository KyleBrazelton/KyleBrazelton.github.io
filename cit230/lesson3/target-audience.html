Target Audience

Everyone from someone looking to know the local weather to those who are looking to make trips to far off destinations. Weather hunter is targeting specifically those who are planning trips, or activities where the weather will play a major role. The age of the average user of weather hunter would be between age (21-50).

Personas

John Doe:

  • Age:32
  • Interest: Hunter/Outdoorsman
  • Plans trips frequently, uses technology to scope the trip.

Jane Doe

  • Age:45
  • Interest: Trips to the park with grandchildren
  • Uses technology, mostly phone/desktop computer.

Scenarios

  • Person planning a camping/hunting trip but needs to know what they will be expecting
  • Wanting to know the daily weather for clothing/apparel purposes
  • Need to know the weather for driving and awareness
  • Going on a boating trip, and need to know weather and wind conditions
  • Knowing the conditions for any/all outdoor activities
